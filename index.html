About Us

Checker Freightlines is a trusted and growing truck dispatching company based in Wilmington, Delaware, with nearly 4 years of proven industry experience. Since our founding in 2021, we've been dedicated to providing reliable, efficient, and timely dispatch solutions for carriers across the United States. With a strong team of dispatch professionals and a customer-first approach, we specialize in connecting drivers with the best freight opportunities, ensuring maximum profitability and smooth operations. Our commitment to integrity, transparency, and responsiveness sets us apart in the fast-paced logistics landscape. Whether you're an owner-operator or a fleet looking to scale, Checker Freightlines is your dependable partner on the road to success.
